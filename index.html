Laboratory Techniques

Some of the known techniques are the following:

  • Vacuum filtration: Prepared the system to separate a solid-liquid mixture using Buchner flask, Büchner funnel, filter paper and suction.
  • Point determination: Determine the melting point of several compounds using a Digi melt. This is a quickly and easy machine to determine the melting point.
  • Drying agent: Utilize drying agents to remove water from an organic solution.
  • Rotary evaporator: developed a separation of solvent using the evaporation of the solvent. Leaving in the round bottom flask just the solute.
  • TLC (thin layer chromatography): Utilized TLC paper to determine the purity of a solution.
  • Titration: developed a system to measure the concentration of a mixture using a burette with base, and acid, beakers, a hot plate and calculate the ph. of the substance using a pH meter.
